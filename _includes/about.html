How It Works

Popular small and traditional shops in Japan often require phone reservations due to limited seats and hours... Don't worry, we can help!

  • Send Us Your Inquiry

    Please fill out the form below and send us your inquiry.

  • We Write You Back

    We will make a phone call on your behalf. If we can secure a seat for you, we will email you the payment link for our service.

    Please complete your payment to us within 30 minutes using a credit card.

  • Visit Your Destination

    Please be there on time. Japanese people are very strict about time.
